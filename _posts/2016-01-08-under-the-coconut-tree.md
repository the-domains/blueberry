# **Under the coconut tree**

You see the ocean come close

A tide rushes up your feet

And you arch your head

To the sky

To drink that coconut milk

# It splashes down your throat

And the sun

Bakes you

Until you're sweet

As a cake

# You found shelter

Hope is abound

Under the coconut tree
